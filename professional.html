Hi, I'm Walter

I am a Colombian mathematician specialized in Hodge theory, algebraic geometry and categorical logic, and interested in its applications, mainly to cryptography and mathematical physics. I received my PhD from IMPA in 2022, under the supervision of Hossein Movasati. I am currently a full-time professor at Politécnico Grancolombiano, in Bogotá. Besides science, languages and phonology are an old passion of mine, and I'm currently learning Cantonese and Mandarin, while continuing my path with English, German, French and Portuguese: speaking a language is like playing an instrument, mostly with your tongue! You'll also find my reflections upon these topics in this page.

Professional experience

I am currently a full-time professor at Politécnico Grancolombiano, in Bogotá, Colombia. I have also taught courses at Universidad Nacional de Colombia, Universidad Distrital Francisco José de Caldas, Uniersidad Colegio Mayor de Cundinamarca and Corporación Universitaria Iberoamericana. I have more than 5 years of professional teaching experience at the university level, with constant excellent semestral evaluations by students and colleagues. I have also worked as a private tutor for mathematics, physics and chemistry at different levels, from high school to university.
